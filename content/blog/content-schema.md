# SnipBid Blog Content Schema

All automated SEO drafts must follow this structure.

## Required frontmatter fields

```yaml
---
title: ""
slug: ""
primaryKeyword: ""
searchIntent: ""
tier: "A"
status: "draft"
metaTitle: ""
metaDescription: ""
internalLinks:
  - "/handyman-quote-software"
  - "/quote-to-invoice-software"
cta: ""
createdAt: ""
updatedAt: ""
---
```

## Writing rules

1. Audience must be handymen and small home service businesses in the U.S.
2. Tone should be practical, clear, specific, and trustworthy.
3. Avoid generic AI fluff.
4. Avoid fake certainty in pricing topics.
5. Pricing articles must mention market / job / location variation.
6. Competitor comparison and legal/licensing topics require extra human review.

## Recommended structure

```md
# H1

## Intro
Directly answer the search query in the first 2–3 paragraphs.

## Section 1

## Section 2

## Section 3

## FAQ
At least 3–5 relevant questions.

## Final CTA
Natural product mention, not hard sell.
```

## Length guidance
- A-tier and B-tier articles: 1000–1400 words
- Template-style or tightly scoped articles: 800–1200 words

## Internal linking rules
Each article should include at least 2 internal link suggestions:
- one to a commercial page
- one to a template or related blog page
